# 👤 {{brand.name}} Account

## Creating Your Account

1. Navigate to the **{{brand.name}}** login page
2. Click **Sign Up** to create a new account
3. Enter your email and choose a password
4. Verify your email address

## Creating Your Organization

After signing up, you'll be guided through onboarding:

1. Enter an **invite code** (provided by your admin or the platform)
2. Choose your **organization name** and **slug** (URL-friendly identifier)
3. Select a **plan** (Starter, Pro, or Enterprise)
4. Your organization is created with you as the **owner**

## 👥 Inviting Team Members

As an owner, you can invite team members:

1. Go to **Admin → Tenants** (if you're the platform admin)
2. Team members can sign up and join your organization via invite codes

## 🔑 Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access — manage everything, including admin features |
| **Admin** | Manage agents, pipelines, projects, integrations |
| **Member** | View and run projects, limited configuration |
