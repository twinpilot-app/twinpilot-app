# 🛒 Marketplace

> 🔜 **Coming soon** — The Marketplace is under active development.

---

## 🎯 What's Coming

The Marketplace will let you:

- 🔍 **Browse** agents and factories published by the community
- 📥 **Install** with one click — agents are copied to your factory
- 📤 **Publish** your own agents and factories (free or paid)
- 🏪 Each organization gets a **Store** with its public presence
- ⭐ **Verified** badge for official/trusted publishers

---

## 🏗️ Current State

The infrastructure is ready:
- ✅ `marketplace_stores` table created
- ✅ `marketplace_listings` with listing types (factory, agent, pipeline)
- ✅ 114 built-in agents listed as FREE in the {{brand.shortName}} store
- 🔜 UI for browsing and installing
