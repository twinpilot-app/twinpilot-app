Core Platform

A factory floor for your org's digital twin.

🏭

Factories

One factory per slice of your business — software house, research lab, content studio, ops team. Each holds its own agents, pipelines, DNA, and credentials. Self-contained, shareable, versioned.

🎨

Studio

Design the twin. Compose agents with persona, contract, and tools. Wire them into pipelines with phases, gates, and SIPOC-aware steps. Edit visually or by file — your call.

🚀

Office

Run projects end-to-end. Dispatch sprints, watch each agent work in real time, approve human gates, inspect artifacts. Stream logs live; resume, retry, or branch from any step.

🧩

Any Niche

Software, research, content, operations, compliance, IoT, finance — the substrate is domain-agnostic. Your agent definitions and pipeline shape teach TwinPilot what the work looks like.

Infrastructure

Every layer is yours. No vendor lock-in.

🧠

LLMs on your terms

Claude, GPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral, Qwen, local models — plug any provider with your own API keys. Pick the model per agent, per step, even per sprint. No tax on your token spend, no proprietary wrapper between you and the provider.

BYOK · multi-provider
💻

Local dev that feels native

Run sprints on your machine using the tools you already pay for — Claude Code, Codex, Aider, Plandex, Goose. The TwinPilot CLI brokers the work to your CLIs and streams results back to the platform. Your subscription, your laptop, your filesystem.

CLI subs + local FS
📦

Storage you own

Artifacts live in your Supabase bucket or your filesystem — never in a platform-owned black box. Push sprint outputs to GitHub repos under the owner you choose; one sprint can fan out to multiple destinations. No vendor data prison.

Bucket · Local · Git
📚

RAG built in

Turn docs, URLs, GitHub repos, and Slack threads into a pgvector-backed knowledge base. Agents search it through MCP tools — no wiring, no bespoke embeddings pipeline. Scope by project, share across factories.

pgvector · MCP
⚡

Autonomy via triggers

Start sprints from the UI, CLI, REST API, or GitHub webhooks. Schedule recurring runs. Auto-push on completion. Pipelines wait on human gates and resume when the signal arrives — your digital twin doesn't sleep just because you do.

Event-driven · scheduled
🛒

Marketplace of agents

Install proven agents and whole factory blueprints from the community store. Publish yours — free or paid. Each listing carries your org's identity so credit and revenue route back cleanly.

Share · remix · earn
🔍

Audit by default

Every agent call, tool invocation, model choice, token cost, and artifact write is logged and replayable. Sprint-scoped audit folders ship alongside your outputs, signed by the run. Compliance reviews stop being archaeology.

Full provenance
🔔

Notifications that act

Telegram messages with inline approve/reject buttons. Webhooks to Slack, Discord, Teams, PagerDuty. In-app bell with realtime deltas. The loop closes where your team already is.

Multi-channel
Control

Transparent by design. Yours to govern.

🛑

Human gates

Insert human-approval steps anywhere in a pipeline. Reviewers approve or reject from Telegram, a signed link, or the dashboard — and the sprint picks up exactly where it paused. No silent drift.

💸

Budgets & guardrails

Per-project token/cost budgets, per-agent model ceilings, timeouts, rejection strategies. A runaway agent hits your ceiling, not your bill.

🔐

Tenant isolation

Row-level security down to every table. Workers authenticate per tenant via JWT, never with a shared service key. One compromised tenant doesn't leak into the rest.

You bring the vision.
